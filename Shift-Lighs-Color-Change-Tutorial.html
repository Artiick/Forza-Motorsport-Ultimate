Installed Requirements (Optional)
Requirement 1:
- Download & Install any vector graphics editing software (ONLY if you plan to make your own color palettes)
Step 1:
- Open SimHub
- Head over into "Dash Studio" and then "Overlays"
Step 2:
- Look for the Tachometer Overlay of your choice
- Click "More" and then "Edit Dashboard"
Step 3:
- Locate the menu voices at the top left of your screen
- Click "Dashboard" at the Menu voices and then "Manage Images"
Step 4:
- Click "Add Image" and a Windows floating window will open to import images
- Navigate to the Path in which you saved the Motorsport Ultimate folder.
- Navigate to the "Shift-Lights-Color-Variants" folder.
- And select the color palette folder you wish to use.
- Select *ALL* the images.
- Click "Open" & Confirm the Overwrite popup window with "Ok", and press the blue "Ok"
- Save the Dashboard and if necessary you may want to restart the Overlay for the changes to take effect
