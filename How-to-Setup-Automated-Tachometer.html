Tachometer "How to". This is the easiest to operate but won't be AS precise
To set the Tachometer correctly, hit the rev limiter a few times within the first 10 seconds of the race. After that, it stops tracking to avoid errors.
Preferred method: Do it while sitting still with the clutch engaged.
Note: Bumps or road imperfections might cause a slight variance (0.2%-0.5%).
- When you jump into a car, wait till you can drive the car and then Rev Bang it a couple of times.
- The Tachometer will remain correct until you change cars (even across sessions or lobbies).
- It resets when you change car allowing you to set it correctly again with the same process.
(When you see full Red RPM Gauge)
- When making upgrades you can still update the RPM Gauge with the same process. Jump into a session, wait until you can drive, Rev Bang it.
